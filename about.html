Outside of conquering the world, I enjoy...

Pens

I own hundreds of unique writing pens! My mom and I even go to local pen shows every year together. Having special pens is an easy conversation starter in meetings and interviews! I confess I probably need a support group for my pen cravings lol.

"The pen is the tongue of the mind" ~Horace

Anything Water

Water is my calming space...if I need to think, I find a lake or a fountain nearby. I love swimming, being on jet skis, on a boat, taking a cruise, the list goes on! If only I drank as much water as I enjoyed it lol!

"Empty your mind, be formless, shapeless - like water." -Bruce Lee

Shopping

A true girly girl inside! Not only do I enjoy walking around the mall for retail therapy, but I also love selling and consignment. I resell my pre-loved handbags and other goodies on eBay, Poshmark and other locations. Helps to turn my own trash into treasure.

"Honestly, shopping beats therapy any time. It costs the same and you get a dress out of it." -Sophie Kinsella
